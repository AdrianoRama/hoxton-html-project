Biography

Adriano (born november 3 ,1992) is an Albanian singer/songwriter, music and record producer. His initial exposure came from a remix he made for Era Istrefi (E dehun) with the alias (Kreizler) and then with his collaboration as an artist with Toquel (Poppa Bottle) which he co-wrote and produced. Later he released "Darling" at the Albanian festival "Kenga Magjike" which he gained alot of exposure. His latest single "Nale" made a very good impression to the Albanian public by staying 49 weeks at the 'Top Awards' 'The Top List' and reaching nr 2 from 100 songs.
